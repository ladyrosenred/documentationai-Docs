## Overview

Workflow In-Spiration revolutionizes scoring for pole and aerial arts competitions. You judge routines, track performances, and generate results with precision and speed. Designed for judges, coaches, and performers, the app replaces paper score sheets with real-time digital tools. Access it via `https://dashboard.example.com` to manage events from preparation to final standings.

The app supports pole, aerial silk, hoop, and trapeze disciplines. You create competitions, assign judges, input scores live, and view leaderboards instantly. Say goodbye to manual calculations and hello to accurate, tamper-proof results.

Workflow In-Spiration ensures fair judging with anonymous scoring and automated ties resolution.
</Callout>

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://dashboard.example.com/signup` and create your account with email verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Event" icon="calendar">
    Navigate to Events > New Event. Enter details like discipline (Pole), date, and max performers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Performers" icon="plus">
    Upload CSV or enter manually: name, routine length, music file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Judges" icon="send">
    Share judge links via email. They access scoring panels upon login.
  </Step>
</Steps>
