## Overview

Follow these steps to launch a digital scoring event.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new event">
    ````
    Click "Create Event" on your Dashboard, then name your event.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add categories and acts">
    ````
    Add performance categories (e.g., Pole, Hoop), then list all acts.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite judges">
    ````
    Go to the Judges tab and send invites (see FAQ for details).
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish event">
    ````
    Click "Publish" so judges and performers can access the schedule and info.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
