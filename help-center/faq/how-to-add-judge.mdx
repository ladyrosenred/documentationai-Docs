## Overview

You can add judges to your event in a few steps.

## Steps

1. Go to your Event Dashboard.
2. Click the "Judges" tab.
3. Select "Add Judge".
4. Enter the judge's name and email.
5. Assign the relevant scoring category.
6. Click "Invite".

The judge receives an email invitation to join and score acts.
