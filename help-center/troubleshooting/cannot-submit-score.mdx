---
title: Cannot Submit Score
description: Fix scoring submission errors.
---

## Symptoms

- Submit button not working
- Message: "Failed to submit score"

## Causes

- Unstable internet
- Required fields not filled

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check internet connection">
    ````
    Ensure your device is online and reconnect if needed.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review score entry">
    ````
    Confirm all required fields are completed before submitting.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the page">
    ````
    Reload your browser and try again.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
