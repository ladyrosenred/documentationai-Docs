## Prerequisites

Before diving in, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- An iOS or Android device running the latest OS version
- A stable internet connection for account creation
- Basic knowledge of pole or aerial arts routines for scoring practice
</Callout>

## Download and Install the App

Workflow In-Spiration is available on both major mobile platforms. Follow the platform-specific instructions below.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open App Store">
        Launch the App Store on your iPhone or iPad.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Search and Download">
        Search for "Workflow In-Spiration" and tap **Get** to download.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch the App">
        Open the app from your home screen once installation completes.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android" icon="android">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Google Play Store">
        Launch the Google Play Store on your device.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Search and Install">

      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch the App">
        Tap **Open** after installation or find it in your app drawer.

    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create Your Account

New users start by creating an account directly in the app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    On the welcome screen, select **Sign Up** instead of logging in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and accept the terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link and click it to activate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the app and sign in with your new credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your Profile

Customize your profile to reflect your judging expertise.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Profile" icon="settings">
    Tap the profile icon in the top-right corner of the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Details" icon="user">
    Enter your name, certification level (e.g., Level 1 Judge), and preferred disciplines like pole or aerial silk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Avatar" icon="image">
    Add a professional photo for competitions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="save">
    Tap **Save** to apply your settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Perform Your First Score Entry

Score a sample routine to get familiar.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start New Score" icon="plus-circle">
    Tap **New Routine** on the dashboard and select the discipline (e.g., Pole Artistic).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Elements" icon="edit">
    Tap elements as they occur: mount (2.0 pts), inversion (3.5 pts), dismount (2.5 pts).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust Difficulty" icon="sliders">
    Modify execution scores for form: 0.5 for minor deductions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Save" icon="check-circle">
    Preview total score, then tap **Submit Score**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Practice with sample routines in the app's demo mode before live events. Your first score unlocks advanced analytics.
</Callout>
